Shield Maiden

A safe play done later in the day.

Strategy

"I am Shield Maiden, and I will not let you down!"

Opening Gambit

At 10:30 look at the direction based on the 10 and 5 minute chart. They should match. Then look at the 1 minute chart going in the same direction. Buy at that time, 1 position. The expiration date should be between 4 days or higher and it should be in the money.

Profit Target

Sell at 20%.

Exit Strategy

Auto sell at 20% gain. You should not see a 1 minute signal in the opposite direction soon after purchasing. If so, restart. Buy another at the next 1 minute signal in the correct direction.

More information

There are plenty of strategies. Try them all!
